This is a desprication of board games

Board games are a type of tabletop game that involves moving, placing, or trading pieces on a specially marked board according to a set of rules. They vary widely in complexity, from simple games of chance to intricate strategy games, and often incorporate elements like dice or cards. Playing board games offers social and cognitive benefits, such as improved problem-solving skills, teamwork, and critical thinking.
